SOFIA

The Speedrun Observer Following Incredible Avatars (S.O.F.I.A.) tracks levels of speedrunners in your guild.

The list sorts players in your guild by their level and by the time they did level up: the highest level are on top of the list and, when two players have the same level, the player who was the first one to level up is on top.

The primary intent is to track Hardcore players who level up in a new guild or a fresh realm.

Known limitations

For now the addon can only track your guildmates and realm friends.

Level up time is taken when you log in. If a player levels up when you are not connected, you will only be informed of this event next time you log in. Because of that, if you do not log in often enough, you may have inaccurate level up times.

Due to how the game client updates the friend list, you will not know when a friend levels up until you log in or until they log in. Reloading your UI doesn't even work, you need to log out and log back in. This is probably an optimization that was necessary 20 years ago to reduce client-server load, yet this is still in effect nowadays.
